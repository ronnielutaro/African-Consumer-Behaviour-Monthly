# East Africa Data

This directory contains regional datasets covering the member states of the East African Community (EAC): Burundi, Kenya, Rwanda, South Sudan, Tanzania, and Uganda.

## Subdirectories

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `economy/` | GDP, GDP per capita, and trade comparisons |
| `demographics/` | Population estimates by country |
| `technology/` | Internet penetration and mobile money adoption by country |

## Primary Sources

- **World Bank Open Data:** https://data.worldbank.org
- **African Development Bank:** https://dataportal.opendataforafrica.org
- **World Bank Global Findex:** https://globalfindex.worldbank.org
- **ITU:** https://www.itu.int/en/ITU-D/Statistics

## Notes

- South Sudan data is incomplete for early years due to the country's independence in 2011
- DRC (Democratic Republic of Congo) became an EAC member in 2022; it will be added in future updates
- All monetary values are in current USD unless otherwise stated

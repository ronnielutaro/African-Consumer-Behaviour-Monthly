# Uganda — Business

Datasets on business formation and investment activity in Uganda.

## Files

| File | Description | Period | Source |
|------|-------------|--------|--------|
| `business_registrations_annual.csv` | New formal business registrations per year | 2006–2022 | World Bank Entrepreneurship Survey / URSB |

## Column Reference

### `business_registrations_annual.csv`
| Column | Unit | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| `year` | YYYY | Calendar year |
| `new_business_registrations` | count | Number of newly registered limited liability companies |

## Notes
- Data reflects formal-sector registrations only; informal businesses are not counted.
- Source: Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB) via World Bank Entrepreneurship Survey.
